1. Bookings Policy

When you make a booking through EduaiTrips, you are entering into a contract directly with the travel service provider (e.g., airline, hotel, tour operator). EduaiTrips acts solely as an intermediary to facilitate these bookings.

  • All bookings are subject to availability and the specific terms and conditions of the respective service providers.
  • It is your responsibility to ensure all passenger details provided during booking match the official government-issued ID exactly.
  • Confirmation emails serve as proof of booking but not necessarily as a final ticket. E-tickets and vouchers will be issued separately.

2. Cancellations & Refunds

Cancellation policies vary significantly depending on the service provider, ticket type, and timing of the cancellation. EduaiTrips applies its own service fee for processing cancellations in addition to any penalties charged by the provider.

Standard Refund Timeline

Eligible refunds are processed back to the original payment method within 7-14 business days after receiving the refund from the respective airline or hotel partner.

3. Visa Service Terms

EduaiTrips provides visa facilitation services but does not guarantee the approval of any visa application. The final decision rests entirely with the respective embassy or consulate.

  • Visa application fees and EduaiTrips service charges are strictly non-refundable, regardless of the application outcome.
  • Users must provide accurate, complete, and authentic documentation as requested by the specific country's embassy.

4. Payment Terms

All payments on EduaiTrips are processed securely through our integrated payment gateway partner, PayU. By making a payment, you agree to their terms of service.
